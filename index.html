Hello! I am James Newling. Welcome to my personal website.

Summary:

In February 2018 I graduated with a PhD from the École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) in machine learning. Prior to that I studied (mostly) mathematics in Europe and South Africa. I enjoy learning and teaching mathematics, algorithm development, AI research, and programming (C++, Python, a bit of html!).

My PhD was advised by Francois Fleuret at the Idiap Research Institute. My thesis was on partitional clustering, a sub-field of unsupervised machine learning. During my PhD I did an internship with AMD, developing fast matrix multiplication kernels, which ignited my interest in specialized hardware for accelerating machine learning algorithms.

As of March 2018 I am a software engineer at Graphcore.
